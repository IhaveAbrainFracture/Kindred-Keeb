# instructions to build
1.using the dowels and such attach the 2 major case and plate parts together then if possible 'melt' then together (like press a iron on both or smthing)
2. in the allocated area insert the insert nuts
3.cut and place the foam in the alloacted area then place the pcb on the groves outlines for it
4. place the fully assembled plate ontop of the pcb before placing all the switches
5. place the plate pcb sandwitch on the case and bolt into the allocated bolt holes and pray
